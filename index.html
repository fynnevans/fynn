Accounting With I.T

There are endless job opportunities in Accounting Information Technology. You can work in the accounting department of a privately owned company or in government or NGO. Any organisation that has any element of billing & taxation involved, needs an accountant. Choose an Accounting Information Technology job that suits you best..

Software Engineering

A career in software engineering sets you up for designing and creating software that powers the world we live in. From computers and mobile phones, to home appliances, vehicles, precision machinery, and so much more, all electronic devices around us are going smart.

Cyber Security

Cyber-crime is rated to be the number one challenge by CIOs globally. It can impact anyone from an individual to an organization or a nation. Cyber Security is important as governments, defence forces, corporates, businesses etc. collect, process, and store unprecedented amounts of data..

Database Technology

Database Technology has come a long way since it was first developed. Especially in the last 2 decades, Database Management Systems have evolved at a very fast pace. A career in database technology opens up unprecedented opportunities through its application in the corporate world for you..

Graphic Designing & Web Development

Graphic Designing & Web Development is the exciting world of effectively conveying information visually. As the world shifts to digital with increasing urgency, design has a more important role now than ever before: to communicate ideas and information, to solve problems creatively, and to connect people.

System Engineering

As the world depends more than ever before on the “Internet of things” (IoT), System Engineering also gets its share of the spotlight. System Engineering is one of the most exciting branches of science today, that brings together design, programming, and technical management..
